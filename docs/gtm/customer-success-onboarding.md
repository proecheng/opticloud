# Customer Success Onboarding

## Purpose

Define a lightweight founder-led onboarding workflow for M4.5 evaluation customers.

## Workflow

| Stage | Owner | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Qualification | Founder | Buyer problem and success metric |
| Technical setup | Engineering / CS | Quickstart completed or API path selected |
| First workflow | CS | One optimization or forecasting sample evaluated |
| Follow-up | Founder / CS | Questions, blockers, and next step recorded |
| Story review | Marketing | Draft customer story only if consent exists |

## Support Boundary

This workflow does not create production SLA, incident response SLA, or legal support commitments. Support terms require legal and commercial approval.

## Data Handling

Do not commit buyer datasets, CRM exports, interview transcripts, screenshots, or private contact evidence. Future evidence must be redacted and validated separately.

## Next Update Trigger

Update after the first two lighthouse interviews produce repeatable onboarding questions.
