## Customer Context

Energy operators evaluate forecasting vendors by forecast error, operational risk, and workflow fit with existing reporting obligations. The buying motion requires conservative claims because penalties, grid dispatch, and regulatory reporting are high-stakes.

## Baseline Pain

- Forecast errors can create penalty exposure.
- Teams need uncertainty bands, not a single overconfident number.
- Procurement teams ask for security posture, data retention, and model accountability before pilot approval.

## OptiCloud Intervention

OptiCloud frames forecasting as an API and spreadsheet-assisted evaluation workflow with P10/P50/P90-style output, risk labels, and buyer-readable explanation. The story remains a draft and does not claim live grid use.

## Expected Outcome

- Faster evaluation of forecast quality against historical samples.
- Clearer buyer conversation around uncertainty, support path, and data handling.
- Early validation of whether energy buyers understand plan-level packaging and Credits.
