# Lighthouse Customer SLO

## Goal

Run at least one lighthouse customer recruitment attempt per month. This is a process SLO, not a revenue forecast or logo commitment.

## Funnel Stages

| Stage | Definition | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Targeted | Segment and buyer problem identified | Internal note, not committed if private |
| Qualified | Buyer has a relevant workflow and evaluation owner | Redacted future report |
| Interviewed | Structured interview completed within one week of qualification | Redacted future report |
| Drafted | Customer story draft created within two weeks after interview | Draft doc if consent allows |
| Blocked | Consent, evidence, data, or legal blocker exists | Redacted blocker summary |

## Cadence

- Monthly: at least one recruitment attempt.
- Within one week after qualification: interview target.
- Within two weeks after interview: draft-story target if consent exists.

## Ownership

- Founder: recruitment and qualification.
- Marketing: draft story and buyer-safe copy.
- Customer Success: onboarding questions and support notes.
- Legal: consent and publication review.

## Evidence Boundary

Future evidence belongs under `reports/gtm-toolkit/<run_id>/` and requires validator support before it can be committed. Do not commit CRM exports, contact lists, recordings, transcripts, screenshots, or private prospect lists.
