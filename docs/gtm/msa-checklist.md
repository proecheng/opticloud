# Master Services Agreement Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist captures topics for legal review before Enterprise work. It is not a contract.

## Required Sections

- Parties and service description.
- Data ownership and data retention.
- Confidentiality and permitted use.
- Security controls and certification status.
- Support and escalation language.
- Billing, taxes, refunds, and termination.
- Liability, dispute resolution, and governing law.

## Boundaries

- Do not claim SOC 2 or ISO certification completion.
- Do not promise production SLA in M4.5 collateral.
- Do not include customer names, logos, or private terms in committed docs.
